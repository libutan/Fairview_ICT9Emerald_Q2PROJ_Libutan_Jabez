Bulalo
Ingredients and Procedures
  1. 2 lbs beef shank
  2. Â½ cabbage whole leaf individually detached
  3. 1 bundle bok choy
  4. 2 cobs corn each cut into 3 parts
  5. 2 Tablespoons whole peppercorn
  6. 1/2 cup green onions cut unto 1 1/2 inch pieces
  7. 1 onion
  8. 34 ounces water
  1. In a big cooking pot, pour in water and make it boil.
  2. Put the beef shank followed by the onion and whole pepper corn then simmer for 1 hour ( add 30 mins if you're using a pressure cooker) or until meat is tender.
  3. Add the corn and make it simmer for another 10 minutes
  4. Lastly, add the fish sauce,cabbage, pechay, and green onion (onion leeks)
  5. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!
