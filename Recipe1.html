Jasmine Garlic Fried Rice
Ingredients and Procedures
  1. 2 cups uncooked jasmine rice
  2. 1 ½ cups water
  3. ½ (15 ounce) can unsweetened coconut cream
  4. 2 teaspoons ground turmeric (Optional)
  5. ¼ teaspoon salt
  6. 3 tablespoons coconut oil
  7. ¼ cup finely chopped garlic
  8. 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  1. Place rice in a large bowl with enough water to cover by 1-3 inches; rinse it for 3 times, then drain after.
  2. Combine rice, 1 1/2 cups water, coconut cream, turmeric, and salt in a saucepan; then wait for it to boil. Lower the heat + simmer until rice is cooked.
  3. Heat coconut oil in a 12-inch skillet or wok over high heat. Add garlic; cook until it just begins to brown, for 2-4 minutes. Add rice and spread in an even layer; cook, without stirring, until lightly browned and crisp on the bottom, for 4 minutes. Lastly Add fish sauce; cook, stirring, until fully combined and heat it, about 2 minutes.
