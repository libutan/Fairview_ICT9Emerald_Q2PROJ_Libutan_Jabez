Champorado
Ingredients and Procedures
  1. 1 cup glutinous sweet rice
  2. 2 cups light coconut milk
  3. ½ cup cocoa powder
  4. 2 teaspoons ground turmeric (Optional)
  5. ¼ teaspoon salt
  6. 1 cup white sugar
  7. 1 teaspoon salt
  8. 1 cup thick coconut milk
  1. Combine rice and light coconut milk in a large pot; bring to a boil for 10 minutes, while stirring occasionally to keep rice from sticking to the bottom of the pot.
  2. Stir cocoa powder, sugar, and salt into rice; reduce heat to low, cover, and continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until rice is tender, about 10 minutes more.
  3. Pour thick coconut milk into rice mixture; stir to combine. Serve hot.
